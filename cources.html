Courses

At Digital Pixel, we offer a range of courses that are designed to help individuals and businesses improve their online presence and succeed in the digital age. Here are some of the courses we offer, along with brief descriptions:

Web Development

Our Website Design and Development course can be tailored to teach website creation using a variety of tools, including WordPress. Here are some more details about how the course covers website creation using WordPress:

  • Introduction to WordPress: The course starts with an introduction to WordPress, covering the basics of how the platform works and how to set up a website.
  • WordPress Themes: Participants will learn how to choose and customise WordPress themes, which determine the overall look and feel of the website.
  • WordPress Plugins: Participants will learn how to use WordPress plugins to add functionality to their website, such as contact forms, galleries, and more.
  • Responsive Design with WordPress: The course covers how to create responsive websites using WordPress, including how to choose a responsive theme and customize the design to work well on all devices.
  • Website Optimization with WordPress: The course covers how to optimize WordPress websites for search engines, including best practices for optimizing content and using plugins.
  • Hosting and Deployment with WordPress: Participants will learn how to host their WordPress website and deploy it to a live server, making it accessible to the public.
SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of optimising your website and content to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). In this course, participants will learn how to improve their website’s visibility in search engines, drive more traffic to their site, and improve their online presence. Our course covers the following topics:

  • Link Building: This involves acquiring backlinks from other reputable websites to improve your website’s authority and search engine rankings.
  • Content Marketing: This includes creating valuable and relevant content to attract and engage your target audience.
  • On-Page Optimization: This includes optimizing your website’s content, structure, and other on-page elements to improve your rankings in search engines.
Social media management

Social media management involves creating, publishing, and managing content on social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn. In this course, participants will learn how to create and implement an effective social media strategy, develop engaging content, and measure the success of their social media efforts. Our course covers the following topics:

  • Social Media Strategy: This includes setting goals, identifying your target audience, and choosing the right social media platforms for your business.
  • Content Creation: This involves creating engaging and shareable content, including text, images, and videos.
  • Social Media Advertising: This includes using social media advertising to reach a larger audience and drive more traffic to your website.
Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising involves paying for clicks on your ads, typically on search engines or social media platforms. In this course, participants will learn how to create and manage effective PPC advertising campaigns to drive targeted traffic to their website and improve conversions. Our course covers the following topics:

  • PPC Strategy: This includes setting goals, identifying your target audience, and choosing the right keywords and ad formats for your campaign.
  • Ad Creation: This involves creating compelling and relevant ads that will attract clicks and conversions.
  • Campaign Optimization: This includes monitoring and optimizing your campaign to improve performance and achieve your goals.
Email Marketing

Email marketing involves sending targeted and personalized emails to your subscribers to promote your products or services. In this course, participants will learn how to create effective email campaigns, build a subscriber list, and track metrics to improve their email marketing efforts. Our course covers the following topics:

  • Email Campaign Strategy: This includes setting goals, identifying your target audience, and creating relevant and engaging email content.
  • List Building: This involves building a quality email list of subscribers who are interested in your products or services.
  • Email Metrics: This includes measuring the success of your email campaigns and using data to optimize your campaigns.
Content marketing

Content marketing involves creating valuable and relevant content to attract and engage your target audience. In this course, participants will learn how to develop a content strategy, create different types of content, and measure the effectiveness of their efforts. Our course covers the following topics:

  • Content Strategy: This includes setting goals, identifying your target audience, and creating a plan for your content marketing efforts.
  • Content Creation: This involves creating different types of content, including blog posts, info graphics, videos, and more.
  • Content Promotion: This includes promoting your content through various channels, such as social media, email, and paid advertising.
